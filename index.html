Data from the NYC Department of City Planning was used to find potential sites for new hospitals in Queens. Potential locations were narrowed down by only considering sites that:
  • Owned by NYC, NYS, or federal government
  • Not owned by NYC DOE, NYCHA, HPD, or Parks
  • Current use is either vacant or parking lot
  • Lot area greater than 20,000 square feet
  • Lot shape not too narrow or shallow
  • Alleys, islands, lots not street accessible, & submerged not included
Decommissioned hospital data found by cross-referencing MapPluto 2002 records with currently licensed NYS DOH hospital facilities Sources

  • NYC DCP MapPLUTO 02b
  • NYS DOH Health Facilities
